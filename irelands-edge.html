Ireland’s Edge: The Podcast

Creative Direction / Audio Reactive Visual Content / 2021

Client: Ireland’s Edge

A series of audio reactive sketches were created for the promotion of the Ireland’s Edge Podcast series over multiple platforms. Ireland’s Edge is a multidisciplinary creative event series that makes up a distinct strand of Other Voices festival of music and ideas. The project involved creating visual clips in Touchdesigner within the theme of the visual style developed by graphic designer Sarah Moloney
