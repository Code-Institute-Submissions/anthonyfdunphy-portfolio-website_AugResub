Tebi Rex—EP 2018

Creative Direction / Scenic Design / 2018

Client: Tebi Rex

Venue: Little Big Tent, Electric Picnic, 2018

Photo Credit : Fionn Murphy

The design for the Tebi Rex performance on the Little Big Tent at Electic Picnic aimed to create a playful and unusual set design. The visual content mimics the group own sense of fun, energetic and unconvential dynamics. The performance weaved between digital compositons both pre-rendered and generative animation from Touchdesigner which were centred around scenic elements and themes.
